Referral Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility

The referral program is available to registered customers of Online Grocery. A referee must be a genuine new customer. A referral code can only be attached before the referee has any paid order on their account.

2. New Customer Discount

  • The referee receives £5 off their first paid order only.
  • The £5 discount is a one-off benefit and cannot be reused.
  • The discount is only available after a valid referral code has been attached to the referee account.
  • The referral code may be entered at sign-up, in the profile area, or at first checkout, provided no paid order exists yet.

3. Referrer Reward

  • The referrer receives £1 referral credit for each paid order placed by that referee.
  • Referral credit stacks automatically on the referrer account.
  • Referral credit is automatically applied at checkout to future orders made by the referrer.
  • Referral credit has no cash value and cannot be transferred, exchanged, or withdrawn.

4. Restrictions

  • Self-referrals are not allowed.
  • A referee can only be linked to one referrer.
  • Once a referral code has been attached to an account, it cannot be changed.
  • Online Grocery may refuse, cancel, or reverse referral benefits where fraud, abuse, duplicate accounts, or misuse is suspected.

5. Order Status and Reversals

  • Referral rewards are created only after a successful paid order.
  • If an order is cancelled, invalidated, or not successfully paid, the referral reward will not be granted.
  • Online Grocery reserves the right to reverse referral credit that was granted in error.

6. Program Changes

Online Grocery may update, suspend, or withdraw the referral program or these terms at any time where reasonably necessary. Any misuse of the program may result in removal of referral benefits.
